Spend your summer learning, building, and making an impact.

At HackCville's immersive Launch summer program you'll become a full-stack developer or full-stack marketer, learn about dynamic industries from experts and changemakers, and learn how to take an idea from conception to a world-changing venture.

Learn more
Apply Now →

Learn in-demand, critical skills.

You'll spend the first half of the summer becoming expert in either software engineering or digital marketing.

No previous experience is required. You'll learn these skills through fun, interactive projects with guidance from our expert instructors.

More Info →

Build broad industry expertise.

Each week you'll explore a different industry through company tours, one-on-one mentorship, interactive workshops, and job shadowing. We'll focus on industries like healthcare, energy, and food.

You'll add industry leaders to your network while gaining a deep understanding of our world's biggest challenges. You'll use this knowledge in the third and final part of the summer.

More Info →

Make solutions that matter.

This is where it all comes together. You'll take the skills and industry knowledge you've gained to start projects with your peers. Maybe you have an idea for how to improve hospital care, or a better way to get nutritious food to the masses.

Our team of experienced entrepreneurs will teach you the entrepreneurial process - how to test an idea, get customers, and manage growth - so you can launch these ideas by the end of the summer.

About Launch & HackCville

Launch is the summer program of HackCville, an entrepreneurial talent accelerator in Charlottesville (Cville), Virginia. We help students of all backgrounds start themselves and their projects. Founded in 2012, HackCville is a 501(c)(3) non-profit led by U.Va. alumni and students. Learn more →
