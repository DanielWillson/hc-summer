Tuition & Scholarships

HackCville's Launch summer program offers a unique combination of skill building, networking, and hands-on projects unlike any summer program available. All of this is included for a dramatically lower cost than comparable programs.

Scholarships are available to assist students with financial need. Financial need has no impact on your application to Launch.

What's Included

  • Full access to the 11-week program curriculum, mentorship, and resources
  • 24/7 access to HackCville's two workspaces on Elliewood Avenue for the duration of the program
  • Upon completion of the program, you'll be granted HackCville membership, giving you advance access to other HackCville programs and exclusive job opportunities
  • There are no additional textbooks to buy or fees to pay after the cost of tuition. You won't have to pay for summer housing either if you already have an apartment in Charlottesville.

About Our Scholarships

Scholarships are available to students with financial need thanks to generous donations from our alumni donors and company sponsors. Some of our scholarships will cover up to 100% of the summer tuition cost.

You'll be asked to note if you have financial need on your application for Launch. If you questions about this, please don't hesitate to email us at launch@hackcville.com.

About Launch & HackCville

Launch is the summer program of HackCville, an entrepreneurial talent accelerator in Charlottesville (Cville), Virginia. We help students of all backgrounds start themselves and their projects. Founded in 2012, HackCville is a 501(c)(3) non-profit led by U.Va. alumni and students. Learn more →
