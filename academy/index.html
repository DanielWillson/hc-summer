Get paid to learn coding, web design, marketing, or data science this summer.

Become a software engineer, web designer, marketer, or data scientist and experience an immersive summer internship at a top Charlottesville startup. Best of all, we'll pay you $1000 to do it, all in 12 weeks this summer.

Apply Now →

Learn in-demand, critical skills.

You'll spend the first half of the summer becoming expert in software engineering, web design, data science, or digital marketing.

No previous experience is required. You'll learn these skills through fun, interactive projects with guidance from our expert instructors.

More Info →

Build broad industry expertise.

Each week you'll explore a different industry through company tours, one-on-one mentorship, interactive workshops, and job shadowing. We'll focus on industries like healthcare, energy, and food.

You'll add industry leaders to your network while gaining a deep understanding of our world's biggest challenges. You'll use this knowledge in the third and final part of the summer.

More Info →

Build experience at a startup internship.

You'll take the skills you've learned from the first half of the program and put them to work at a local startup internship. We've partnered with top companies across Charlottesville to give you hands-on experience and real-world projects.

You'll walk away with new skills and new experience to launch you toward whatever's next.

About Launch & HackCville

Launch is the summer program of HackCville, an entrepreneurial talent accelerator in Charlottesville (Cville), Virginia. We help students of all backgrounds start themselves and their projects. Founded in 2012, HackCville is a 501(c)(3) non-profit led by U.Va. alumni and students. Learn more →
