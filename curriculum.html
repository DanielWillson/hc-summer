Launch Program Outline

Launch is an 11-week program, split into 3 parts:

  1. Skills training
  2. Industry immersion
  3. Launching a project

You’ll meet at HackCville’s coworking space each day 9:30am-5:30pm starting on May 29 and ending on August 4. Here’s how it will work.

Part 1/3: Skills Training

You’ll pick one of two tracks to immerse yourself in, each led by one of our expert instructors.

Track A: Software Engineering

Our software engineering track will train you to become a full-stack developer in a fraction of the time of a traditional computer science program. You’ll learn through fun, challenging, real hands-on projects - no theoretical stuff here. Comparable programs can cost upwards of $10,000 for just this portion of our program. No former computer science experience is required.

About Launch & HackCville

Launch is the summer program of HackCville, an entrepreneurial talent accelerator in Charlottesville (Cville), Virginia. We help students of all backgrounds start themselves and their projects. Founded in 2012, HackCville is a 501(c)(3) non-profit led by U.Va. alumni and students. Learn more →
