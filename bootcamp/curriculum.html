Launch Bootcamp Curriculum

Launch is an immersive 9-week program that covers two major topics:

  1. Deep skills training in marketing, data science, or web development
  2. Professional development and network building

The program starts Monday, June 12. For the first 3 weeks the program is 20 hours / week, and the assignments can mostly be completely remotely. You will meet with the HackCville staff in-person a few times a week.

The next 6 weeks are much more intensive and immersive. You'll meet at HackCville's awesome coworking spaces each weekday 8am-5pm starting on July 3 and ending on August 11. (Learn more about our fun and functional spaces here.)

Part 1/2: Deep Skills Training

You'll pick one of three tracks to immerse yourself in, each led by a cohort of expert instructors.

Track A: Full Stack Development

Our Full-Stack Development Track will move you up the software engineering ladder in a fraction of the time of a traditional computer science program. You'll learn through fun, challenging, real hands-on projects - no theoretical stuff here. Comparable programs can cost upwards of $10,000 for just this portion of our program. No former computer science experience is required.

Here's what you'll learn.

This is an intensive program to turn an individual into a competent, professional software developer. The skills learned in this program will extend far beyond web or mobile development. Trainees learn the ins and outs of the internet, how to design for millions, how to stay up to date with the latest tools, and how to present yourself and sell your ideas.

Here's what you'll walk away with.

This program will include the development of several portfolio-worthy applications. It will ensure a basic understanding of client interactions and consulting. Lastly, you'll be able to take an idea from abstract to implementation in a team environment. It is our aim not only to teach you to make your ideas happen, but also to be both employable and billable as a software development consultant immediately following this program.

Track B: Design & Digital Marketing

"The Valley skill set that should be in highest demand and greatest scarcity is neither engineering nor design, but rather internet marketing." - Dave McClure, Founder, 500Startups

In the Full Stack Marketing Track we aim to teach a combination of art, design, tools, and strategies that make digital marketing a compelling field for study and employment. You'll get trained on the marketing concepts used by some of the most compelling internet companies by people who've done it. Comparable programs cost $10,000 just for this portion of the program.

Here's what you'll learn.

This is an intensive program centered on teaching the tools and mindset you'll need to launch real, integrated, digital marketing programs. You'll learn how marketing is combined with product development, usability, and customer lifetime value to create amazing programs that cut through clutter and make a difference.

Here's what you'll walk away with.

You'll leave with the tools and tactics to launch online products. You'll be well versed in the Adobe Creative Suite, email marketing software, marketing concepts and jargon, lifetime customer value analysis, data analysis, social media campaign creation and management, search engine optimization, and growth hacking strategies. It is our aim that after completing this program you could be a key member of a startup marketing team or well paid digital marketing consultant.

Track C: Data Science

We are flooded with data. Companies are flooded with even more of it. Making sense of data and predicting future trends is one of the most in demand skills on the planet. Come learn why data scientists have "the world’s best job."

In the Data Science Track we aim to teach the art and science of finding, cleaning, understanding, storing, manipulating and visualizing data. Further, we hope you'll go farther and learn how to deploy neural nets and use machine learning to solve real problems. You'll get trained on data science techniques used by people at the forefront of this in-demand industry. Comparable programs cost $10,000 just for this portion of the program.

Here's what you'll learn.

This is an intensive program centered on teaching the tools and mindset you'll need to find, clean, understand, and manipulate data. You'll learn how to create predictive models used by organizations of all types. Utilizing Python and R, you will be able to create applications for everything from automating customer service to predicting customer behavior on ecommerce websites. Further, you’ll learn how to create SQL database structures and visualize data using D3.

Here's what you'll walk away with.

You'll leave with the tools and tactics manipulate large data sets in valuable ways. You'll be well versed in R, Python, statistical analysis, regression models, and predicting the future. Kidding. You will be able, though, to make compelling predictive models that help make sense of our world. It is our aim that after completing this program you could be a key member of a startup marketing, operations, or product development team.

Part 2/2: Professional Development

We'll help you take the next steps toward your career goals.

Beyond the hard skills training, we will work with you to tweak your pitch to companies and find a job you'll love.

What will I learn?

  • How to craft a mission statement to bring clarity and confidence to your career hunt
  • The tools to uncover unique opportunities at wildly innovative compaies
  • How to create a powerful online identity which is key to communicating value to employers.
  • How to go beyond the resume and cover letter and use the new ways to connect with and build relationships with employers
