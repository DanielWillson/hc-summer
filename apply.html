Apply to Launch

If you haven't already, we strongly recommend reading over our Payment page for details on payment and what's included.

If you're ready to apply, our easy application process is detailed below. Please feel free to reach out to launch@hackcville.com if you have any questions!

Step 1: Apply

To apply to Launch, you must:

  • be a UVA student (grad or undergrad) or summer/full-time Charlottesville resident,
  • be 18 years old or older,
  • provide your own laptop computer for the duration of the program, and
  • commit to the full 12 week program, May 22, 2017 - August 11, 2017.

Applications close on February 14 at 11:59pm. Current HackCville members receive extra consideration on their application.

If you're ready to begin your application, you may do so here.

Step 2: Interview

We'll hold interviews with all applicants on a rolling basis, so the earlier you apply, the better. We will notify accepted applicants by February 25. We may offer a second application deadline (March 14) if additional spots become available as we confirm internships with companies, but we strongly recommend applying earlier.

Step 3: Enroll

Applicants will be notified by February 25 if they are accpeted or waitlisted. If you are accepted, you'll have one week to submit at $250 deposit to confirm your spot. You'll get this back in full at the end of the summer. (This deposit can be reduced for students with significant financial need.)

Accepted students will be paid their $1000 stipend in two $500 payments: one 9 weeks into the program, and the final payment at the end of the program.

About Launch & HackCville

Launch is the summer program of HackCville, an entrepreneurial talent accelerator in Charlottesville (Cville), Virginia. We help students of all backgrounds start themselves and their projects. Founded in 2012, HackCville is a 501(c)(3) non-profit led by U.Va. alumni and students. Learn more →
