Apply to Launch

If you haven't already, we strongly recommend reading over our Tuition page for details on payment, scholarships, and what's included.

If you're ready to apply, our easy application process is detailed below. Please feel free to reach out to launch@hackcville.com if you have any questions!

Step 1: Apply

To apply to Launch, you must:

  • be a UVA student (grad or undergrad) or summer/full-time Charlottesville resident,
  • be 18 years old or older,
  • provide your own laptop computer for the duration of the program, and
  • commit to the full 12 week program, May 22, 2017 - August 11, 2017.

Applicants are accepted on a rolling basis until the program is full.

The written application (available here) is comprised of a few essays and short answers, and shouldnâ€™t take more than an hour to complete. We assess candidates on three things: one, two and three.

When you apply determines your final cost of tuition:

  • Apply before February 14 - Final Tuition is $3500
  • Apply before March 14 - Final Tuition is $3750
  • Apply before May 1 - Final Tuition is $4000

Current HackCville members receive a 10% discount on all tuition. Get more details on our tuition, scholarship opportunities, and what's included here.

If you're ready to begin your application, you may do so here.

Step 2: Interview

After you submit your application, you'll hear back from us within 48 hours (weekends not included). We may request an interview. The goal of the interview is to make sure the program is the right fit for you and to answer any questions you have.

Step 3: Enroll

After your interview, you'll hear back from us within 24 hours if you are accepted into Launch. If you are accepted, you will have 7 days to submit your 10% deposit to reserve your spot in Launch. Your deposit is fully refundable before 11:59pm March 13, 2017. After that date deposits are non-refundable.

Your final tuition balance is due by Tuesday, May 2, 2017. If you do not complete Launch in full due to a personal matter or because you are dissatisfied wit the program, you will receive a pro-rated refund based on the number of weeks completed.

About Launch & HackCville

Launch is the summer program of HackCville, an entrepreneurial talent accelerator in Charlottesville (Cville), Virginia. We help students of all backgrounds start themselves and their projects. Founded in 2012, HackCville is a 501(c)(3) non-profit led by U.Va. alumni and students. Learn more →
